History

Snaker is an American distribution company specializing in Sneakers. Our group was created in 2021 in order to allow easy access for all. Snaker stores cater to a 15-25 year-old urban clientele by offering the best of major brands. Thanks to its selection of sneakers for men, women and children, Snaker meets the expectations of an entire generation.

Our Values

Snaker speaks to everyone and shares his passion for sneakers, it's not only a trend, it's a true state of mind that transcends generations. The brand wishes to open up the sneaker and invites all consumers to share its universe. Snaker is committed to a continuous improvement approach to detect and prevent corruption risks. Our suppliers are also committed to transparent business practices. The Snaker Group has implemented an ethical alert system enabling employees and its partners to report any breach of the code of conduct or the law in general.
